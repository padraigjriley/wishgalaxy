ABOUT

The Wish Space is a collaborative project between us and you. The project began as an exploration of our hopes and dreams during this time of global health pandemic and socio-political crises.

We wanted to recreate the moment of daring hope one experiences when tossing a coin into a fountain. In breaking down the mechanics of that moment, we began to ask a different question: what does the act of making a wish offer? A moment to reflect on desire and possibility? A prompt of imagination? An exercise in hope?

Whether at home and in isolation or rebuilding and reopening, we collectively face the loss and failings of humankind.

What will be our new normal? What do we wish for our country and our world? Can we start to re-imagine whatâ€™s possible?

We offer this experience as a gift of self-exploration, solidarity, and community. May it nourish and support us.

With love,

Warther Dixon.

THE COIN

Traditionally, coins have been tossed into wells and fountains as on offering to the presiding water deity. In exchange, she becomes the custodian of the wish, considering with wisdom the terms by which it may come true.

The coin that we give you is precious and powerful. It is your intention distilled, imagination realized and will directed into motion. Place your hope into the coin as you release it into the well.

WISH GALAXY

The Wish Galaxy is a collective capture of global wishes sent to live amongst the stars. Replenishing continuously and anonymously. For every star there is another wish: an infinite unity of hope.
