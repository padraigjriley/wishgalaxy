ABOUT

TheWish.Space is an ongoing interactive artwork by South African artist duo Warther Dixon.

The project began as our answer to how a Wishing Well could exist in virtual space. It has since evolved into a question: What can the act of making a wish offer? A moment to reflect on desire and possibility? A prompt of imagination? An exercise in hope? We offer this experience to you as a gift.


THE COIN

Traditionally, coins have been tossed into wells and fountains as an offering to the presiding water deity. In exchange, she becomes the custodian of the wish, considering with wisdom the terms by which it may come true.

The coin that we offer to you is precious and powerful.

WISH GALAXY

The Wish Galaxy is a collective capture of global wishes sent to live amongst the stars. Replenishing continually and anonymously, for every star there is another wish: an infinite unity of hope.
